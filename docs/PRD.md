# SkillSync AI

## Overview
SkillSync AI is an AI-powered career recommendation platform that helps users analyze their skills, upload resumes, receive personalized recommendations, and generate learning roadmaps.

---

## Problem Statement
Many students and junior developers do not know:
- what skills to learn
- which career path suits them
- what they are missing for target roles

SkillSync AI solves this using AI and recommendation systems.

---

## Target Users
- CS students
- Data Science students
- Fresh graduates
- Junior developers
- Career switchers
